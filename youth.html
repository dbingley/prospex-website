Youth Programmes

What?

We provide programmes offering a safe space and place where young people can access positive activities, workshops, personal development and support.
We often use a community centre located in the centre of a ward which that particular youth programme session is targeted.

Who?

We aim to attract local young people to where the youth programmes are situated. We predominantly encourage those we meet on the streets to attend though, not exclusively those young people.
We encourage young people to refer friends and others to our youth programmes, and Prospex in general.

How?

We aim to see those young people met through our Street Teams become regular attendees.
We provide young people with the opportunity to attend workshops and gain new skills.
We look to engage young people in positive activities whilst in a safe environment.
